MATH SQUAD SPACE MATH ACADEMY — PRIVACY POLICY 🔒 ZERO DATA COLLECTED · FULLY OFFLINE · COPPA COMPLIANT WHAT WE COLLECT Nothing. Math Squad does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information — not from children, not from parents, not from anyone. Game data (scores, progress, session history) is saved locally on your device only, using your device's built-in storage. It never leaves your device. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA) Math Squad is designed for children ages 5–10. We take children's privacy seriously and comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect any personal information from children We do not require account creation or registration We do not use advertising of any kind We do not include social features or chat We do not connect to the internet during gameplay We do not share data with any third parties DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE The following is saved locally on your device and is never transmitted: Pilot name (optional, entered by parent/child) High scores and unlocked ships Session history (dates, scores, accuracy) Difficulty preferences You can delete all stored data at any time from the Parent Zone → Reset All Data option, or by deleting the app. IN-APP PURCHASES Math Squad offers a single optional in-app purchase ("Full Mission Access") to unlock additional game modes. This purchase is processed entirely by Apple and is protected by a parental gate (a math problem that requires adult-level arithmetic to solve). We do not receive or store any payment information. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES Math Squad uses no third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. All fonts are bundled locally within the app. No external network requests are made during gameplay. PARENTAL RIGHTS Since we collect no personal information, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete beyond what is stored on your own device. To remove all game data, use Parent Zone → Reset All Data or delete the app from your device. Contact Questions or concerns?
